Larkspur

Grand & Lovely

Luxurious Larkspur is fabricated in a gorgeous combination of French Lace and graceful Silk Satin Organza.  The beautifully beaded and artfully ribboned drop waist bodice is complimented with flowing Silk Organza sash.   Larkspur's full A-line skirt offers great presence and a touch of tradition to this decadent style.  Made by hand in San Francisco.
